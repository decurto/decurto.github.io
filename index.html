Dr. De Curtò y DíAz

J. de Curtò is a researcher at the Centre for Intelligent Multidimensional Data Analysis (CIMDA) in HK Science Park. He is also with Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV) and Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC). He has been Senior Research Assistant at City University of Hong Kong and Graduate Research Assistant at ETH Zürich. He pursued graduate studies at City University of Hong Kong and Carnegie Mellon. De Curtò also holds a 5-year degree in Telecommunications from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC) and Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB).

Research

I'm broadly interested in computer vision and learning. I develop models that extract high-level information of the world to assist robots and automated systems such as self-driving cars and drones.
